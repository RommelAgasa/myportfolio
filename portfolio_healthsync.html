This is a capstone project titled HealthSync — a comprehensive healthcare management system designed to streamline patient services and enhance clinic operations. The system features an intuitive Patient Registration module for quick onboarding, a reliable Appointment Scheduling tool to reduce wait times, a secure Patient Medical Records database for accurate and accessible health information, and an SMS Alerts feature to keep patients informed about their appointments and important updates.
